# 📦 StockHome

**Know what's in your pantry without opening it.**

StockHome is a home inventory app for your pantry, fridge, and bathroom. It answers civilization's oldest questions: *Do I have toilet paper? How many canned tomatoes is too many? Why do I own four opened jars of mustard?*

Built with Lean principles by someone who applies Toyota Production System thinking to his sock drawer. No shame.

## Features

- 📋 **Track everything** — quantities, categories, photos, storage notes
- ⏰ **Best-before radar** — expiring items get flagged before they become science experiments
- 📷 **Barcode scanning** — scan groceries with your phone camera. Scan a known item and stock goes +1. *Beep.*
- 🛒 **To buy list** — check items off in the store, then move them straight into inventory. The loop closes itself.
- 🔍 **Stock check mode** — standing in aisle 7 wondering if you have dish soap at home? Scan it. Now you know.
- 🌙 **Dark mode only** — because it's designed like a well-organized pantry at dusk, and because we said so


## Privacy

Your data never leaves your phone. Everything lives in your browser's local storage — there's no server, no account, no analytics, no cloud. The only entity that knows you own 14 cans of chickpeas is you.

(Do export a backup occasionally. Local storage is loyal, not immortal.)
